⚠️  ACCOUNT DELETION

Delete Your Account

Permanently remove your VisionRise account and all associated data.

🚨

This action is permanent and cannot be undone

Once your account is deleted, your profile, course progress, certificates, and all personal data will be permanently removed. Please make sure you want to proceed before submitting a request.

📱

Delete via the App (Recommended)

1
Open VisionRise

Launch the VisionRise app on your Android device.

2
Go to Settings

Tap the Settings icon from your dashboard or profile menu.

3
Select Account

Scroll down to the Account section and tap Delete Account.

4
Confirm Deletion

Enter your PIN when prompted and confirm your decision. Your account will be queued for permanent deletion.

✉️

Delete via Email Request

1
Send an email to our support team

Email us at support@visionrise.com

2
Use subject: "Account Deletion Request"

Include your registered email address and full name in the email body.

3
We'll process your request

Our team will verify your identity and process the deletion within 7 business days. You'll receive a confirmation email when complete.

ℹ️
We may ask you to verify your identity before processing the request to protect your account security.
🗑️

Data That Will Be Deleted

Profile InformationFull name, email address, phone number, profile picture, and bio

Authentication DataLogin credentials, OTP history, and third-party OAuth tokens (Google / Apple)

Learning ProgressCourse enrollments, video watch history, quiz scores, and assignment submissions

Certificates EarnedAll certificates and achievements associated with your account

CommunicationsMessages, forum posts, and interactions within group courses or live classes

Uploaded Content (Instructors)Course videos, notes, assignments, and webinar schedules

Device & Usage DataIP address logs, device identifiers, and platform usage metrics

📋

Data That May Be Retained

Certain data may be retained for a limited period as required by law or for legitimate business purposes:

Financial & Transaction RecordsFee payment records and billing history — retained for up to 5 years as required by Indian financial regulations (GST compliance)

Legal & Dispute RecordsAny data relevant to ongoing disputes or legal obligations — retained until the matter is fully resolved

Anonymized AnalyticsAggregated, non-identifiable usage statistics that cannot be linked back to your identity

🔒
All retained data is securely stored, access-restricted, and will be purged as soon as the retention period expires.
